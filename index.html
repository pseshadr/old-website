PORTRAIT

creates software that delivers a comprehensive picture of complex industrial assets. This is crucial for making well-informed decisions pertaining to asset maintenance, leasing, sales, repair and overhaul.

AI + DIGITAL TWINNING

Artificial intelligence (AI) is poised to usher a new era in digital twinning. AI can aid in the creation of more physically representative digital twins, which are crucial for maximising value from expensive assets.

To this end, we develop advanced computer vision and pattern recognition models to rapidly ingest and synthesise heterogenous data. We pair this with our expertise to understand the data, it's uncertainties, and drivers. Our advanced data management system efficiently compresses and stores this processed data -- making it easy for access and analysis. We utilise this to build fleet-wide Bayesian machine learning models that can provide enhanced quantitative insight in an uncertain world.
