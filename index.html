WHAT WE DO

PORTRAIT

creates software that delivers a comprehensive picture of the health of aircrafts and engines. This is crucial for making well-informed decisions pertaining to asset leasing, sales, maintenance and overhaul. Portrait is proudly original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) organisation independent, giving us a unique position in the market as an unbiased company.

AI + MRO

Artificial intelligence (AI) is poised to usher a new era in MRO. AI can aid in the creation of more physically representative digital twins, which are crucial for maximising value from expensive assets.

To this end, we develop advanced computer vision and pattern recognition models to rapidly ingest and synthesise heterogenous data, including borescope videos, asset sensor data, environmental conditions, and maintenance logs of assets. We pair this with sound engineering judgement to understand the data, it's uncertainties, and drivers. Our advanced data management system efficiently compresses and stores this processed data -- making it easy for access and analysis. We utilise this to build fleet-wide Bayesian machine learning models that can provide enhanced quantitative insight in an uncertain world.
