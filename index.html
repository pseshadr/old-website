WHAT WE DO

PORTRAIT

creates software that delivers a comprehensive picture of aviation assets such as aircrafts, engines and landing gears. This is crucial for making well-informed decisions pertaining to asset maintenance, leasing, sales, repair and overhaul. Our company is proudly original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) organisation independent. This gives us a unique position in the market as an unbiased actor.


AI + ASSET LEASING, TRADING, & MRO

Artificial intelligence (AI) is poised to usher a new era in aviation asset management, engineering and finance. Sound statistical methodologies paired with engineering expertise and advanced software tools can offer digital records of each asset with future predictions. Our rich, unparalleled picture of the asset offers new vistas for value-added services and provides greater clarity on the state of the asset.
