LABS

The labs are a dedicated space for us to do our research in. We experiment with new artificial intelligence techniques including advanced algorithms in computer vision and hierarchical Bayesian machine learning. The proprietary tools we develop build upon decades of work done on open-source packages. As a result, our people routinely contribute actively to various open-source projects, including equadratures, pytorch and numpyro.
