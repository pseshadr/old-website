PRODUCTS

Information on our products and services are given below.



PORTRAIT EDGE


This software tool is purpose-built for heterogenous data ingestion and management. We recognise that asset value can be better quantified by fusing information from multiple sources, coming in at different rates. This includes borescope videos, environmental and atmospheric conditions, maintenance logs and part replacements, and sensor / performance data. Not only does this tool serve as a foundational building block for Fleet-wide analysis, but it is useful in its own right to technicians, MROs, OEMs, consultants and lessors.
❮ ❯

PORTRAIT FLEET


This software tool comes in a fully customisable interface. It generates fleet-wide hierarchical models that offer more precise and accurate remaining useful life estimates, scrap rate values, and maintenance work scope planning guidance. Underneath the hood it exploits the shared experiences of different assets of the same family, i.e., CFM56-5B engines to inform more rigorous quantitative estimates.

PORTRAIT LEDGER


Ledger represents our tools for assisting companies working focused on incorporating blockchain technologies within their asset maintenance and workscope ecosystem.

PORTRAIT WORKSHOPS


Bespoke, customer-driven workshops for problem scoping and preliminary project assessments.
