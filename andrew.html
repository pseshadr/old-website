ANDREW DUNCAN, PHD




Andrew Duncan is the Founder and Head of Machine Learning of Portrait Technology. Andrew has over 15 years' experience in research and product development of statistics and machine learning tools across the aerospace, maritime, energy and technology sectors. He is concurrently a Data-Centric Engineering Lecturer in the Statistics Section of the Department of Mathematics at Imperial College London..

He obtained his PhD in Applied Mathematics from the University of Warwick.
