29 MAY 2021 | Our experience with the Olympus IPLEX NX



Our Head of Engineering, Pranay, using the IPLEX NX.

We've recently got access to an Olympus IPLEX NX borescope! These borescopes are used extensively for qualitative and quantitative visual inspections of jet engine components. Having the borescope not only permits us to get a better feel of how the hardware can be used, but also gives us a comprehensive insight into the software that underpins the borescope. This is vital in being able to develop more precise digital twins that represent the state of the jet engine being scanned.
