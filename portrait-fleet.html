PORTRAIT FLEET


This software product comes in a fully customisable interface. It generates fleet-wide hierarchical models that offer more precise and accurate remaining useful life estimates, scrap rate values, and maintenance work scope planning guidance. Underneath the hood it exploits the shared experiences of different assets of the same family to inform more rigorous quantitative estimates.
